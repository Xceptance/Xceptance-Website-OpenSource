Our interns are computer science or business informatics students looking for an interesting part-time job or a topic they would like to talk about in their BA, MA, or diploma thesis. Whatever your personal motivation, we are more than willing to support you in your academic career and offer future jobs if both of us feel we want to work together full-time.

Your Responsibilities and Tasks

  • Create and execute test cases
  • Review bug entries and tickets
  • Retest fixed functionalities
  • Create test tools (optional)
  • Automate tests (optional)

Minimum Qualifications

  • B.A. student in computer science, business informatics, or the like
  • Programming skills (e.g. Java, JavaScript, Scala, Ruby, C, PHP)
  • Basic English and German language skills

Desired Skills

  • Positive attitude with a drive to learn
  • Commitment and reliability
  • Ability to work in a team and dynamic environment
  • Good communication skills
  • Attention to detail and appreciation of quality assurance
