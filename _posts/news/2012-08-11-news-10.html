We are more than happy to announce that we are an official sponsor of the 4th BarCamp Mitteldeutschland held on October 13/14, 2012 in Jena.

Because we think it's a good thing to foster interesting talks and discussions about everything that might be related to our profession, we are sponsoring the 4th Barcamp Mitteldeutschland. It will take place on October 13/14, 2012, in the "Villa am Paradies" and we encourage all attendees to share information and experiences. Attendence is, of course, free of charge. If you are interested, simply go to Barcamp Mitteldeutschland and sign up there.
